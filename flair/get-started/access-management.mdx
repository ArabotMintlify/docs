title: "Access Management"
description: "Flair Access Management"
---

**Flair is designed to support:**

- **Multiple organizations**
- Each organization has **multiple projects**
- Each **project** contains isolated **apps** and shared organization **data**
- Role-based **access control** is enforced at both:
  - **Organization Level**
  - **Project level**
  - **App level **
